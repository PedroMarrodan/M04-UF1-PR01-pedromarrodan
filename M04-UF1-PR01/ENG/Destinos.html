1- Barcelona, Cataluña

Barcelona is not only one of the most beautiful cities in Spain, but also one of the most visited thanks to its monuments, its culture, its people,... It has an ideal Mediterranean climate, which makes it one of the most visited cities in Spain, attracting millions of tourists every year. There are endless activities to do, as well as places which are among the 48 places declared a World Heritage Site in Spain, such as the Palau de la Música Catalana, the Hospital de Sant Pau and several works by Antonio Gaudí.

2- Madrid, Madrid

As it could not be otherwise, among the main destinations you should go to is Madrid. The capital of Spain offers us great historical prestige, with a wide variety of monuments, squares and a great cultural offer that make it one of the most visited places in the country. The number of activities and things to do are so many that you will never get tired of visiting it, always taking new routes and discovering new things.

3- Granada, Andalucía

Granada is, without a doubt, one of the best cities to see in Spain, this being a place that we should all visit at least once in our lives. It has a great culture and history among its neighborhoods and monuments, having a large number of icons of medieval architecture that date back to the occupation of the Moors in ancient Al Ándalus, showing you the Muslim culture and its legacy in its best version. In it is the most visited monument in the country, the Alhambra, mobilizing some 3.5 million people a year who travel to visit it It also has some beautiful neighborhoods, a magnificent gastronomy that, together with its wonderful and characteristic atmosphere, makes this one of the most attractive cities in the country.

4- A Coruña, Galícia

Another very good option to visit is A Coruña. This Galician city is ideal for walking and enjoying, with beaches and outstanding monuments in the center. It has a large number of highly prestigious museums such as the Fine Arts Museum, the House of Sciences, the Domus or the Archaeological Museum, in the Castillo de San Antón. One of the most unforgettable experiences that the visitor can take away from A Coruña, and from Galicia, is the contemplation of a sunset from the Tower of Hercules. The wonderful views and the sunset in front of the only Roman lighthouse in the world still in operation will make you fall in love with Galicia.

5- Tarragona, Catalunya

Tarragona is another of the cities declared a World Heritage Site in Spain. It has a great legacy from Roman times, at which time it was one of the most important cities in Hispania. From this period you can visit several monuments: the Roman Circus, the Amphitheater, the Pont del Diable aqueduct and the Roman Forum. We also recommend you stroll through the narrow streets of its historic center and enjoy monuments such as the Tarragona Cathedral, as well as enjoy Catalan culture.

6- Palma, Mallorca

On the island of Mallorca (Balearic Islands), and on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, is the city of Palma. This has some beautiful beaches and coves where you can enjoy a relaxing holiday with a lot of sun. In addition, it is the second largest cataloged historic center in Europe, only surpassed by that of Rome. You can also visit one of the few castles with a circular floor plan in the world, the Bellver Castle, as well as its beautiful Gothic cathedral, a part of which was renovated by Antonio Gaudí himself.

7- Santander, Cantábria

On the shores of the Cantabrian Sea, we find the city of Santander, which is recognized as one of the most elegant cities in Spain. With its impressive beaches and gulfs you will be able to enjoy wonderful coastal vacations where you will be able to visit very outstanding places such as the Magdalena Peninsula along with its due palace, the Sardinero area and much more!

8- San Sebastian, País Vasco

As we said with Santander, San Sebastián, also on the shores of the Bay of Biscay, is one of the most beautiful cities in Spain, where you can enjoy beautiful stately buildings which flood its streets and invite you to walk through them calmly. In addition, it has one of the most beautiful urban beaches in the country, known as Playa de la Concha, making this one of the most essential destinations to see in the Basque Country. You can also enjoy the endearing Basque culture along with its gastronomy, which you should not miss!
